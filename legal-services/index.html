Legal Services


The team at Wilckens Roche knows that success starts with understanding and requires innovative strategies, not just reactive solutions.  With a comprehensive knowledge of our legal practice areas, Wilckens Roche is ready to put its wealth of experience to work for you.  Our vision is to address the legal challenges you may encounter in achieving your business and personal goals. Wilckens Roche Pty Ltd has developed and implemented a Business Management System (BMS) in order to:

  • document the firm’s business practices;
  • better satisfy the requirements and expectations of its clients; and
  • continually improve the overall management of the firm.

The firm is committed to meeting the requirements of the international standard LAW 9000 for the design, development, production and servicing of its products and services.
